What we collect

  • To generate reflections, the text of an entry is processed by our AI provider ([CONFIRM: Anthropic Claude API]) under commercial terms that do not permit training on your data. [CONFIRM current provider terms.]

Children's information

Liber accounts are held by parents and guardians — children do not hold accounts. The records you create about your children belong to your family. We never publish, share or use children's photos or learning records except to provide the service to you, and we apply our strictest handling to this data.
