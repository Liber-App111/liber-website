Keep the whole picture, together

A project at one house, a museum trip from the other, and no shared place to keep any of it — so nobody has the full picture, and the parent who needs to show progress is missing half of it.

Liber gives your child's learning one home even when your child has two. Both parents can capture the moments they're part of, so the record reflects everything your child is actually doing — not just the half that happened on your days. When a review comes around, or when you simply want to see how they're going, the evidence is complete and in one place.

Built because you asked

This isn't a feature we bolted on. Separated and co-parenting families asked for it, again and again — so we're building Liber to serve you properly, not as an afterthought.
