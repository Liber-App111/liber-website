How it actually works

This isn't a journaling app. It's a way of learning to see.

You capture something ordinary from today. Liber helps you see what it was really about. Over time, a clear picture of how your child learns quietly takes shape.

  • Looking closely enough at the world to want to record it, in their own way.
  • Sitting with something quietly, all on their own steam, because it mattered to them.
  • Choosing, without being asked, to turn a morning into something they made.

Common questions

Questions parents actually ask

Is Liber a curriculum or lesson-planning tool?

No. Liber is deliberately not a curriculum tool. It helps you see and keep the learning already happening in everyday moments — no worksheets, no grades, no lesson plans.

Can Liber help with homeschool registration or a home-visit review?

Yes. The moments you capture quietly build into a record. Liber+ can turn them into a clear, organised story for registration, reviews or an Authorised Person visit — without it ever feeling like a compliance exercise.

Is Liber free?

Yes — capturing moments and building your record is free. Liber+ adds long-range patterns, richer learning stories, full history and review-ready exports.

Who is Liber for?

Relaxed, interest-led, unschooling and neurodivergent homeschool families across Australia, New Zealand and beyond, who want a calm, honest record of real learning.
